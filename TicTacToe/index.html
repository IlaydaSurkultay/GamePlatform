Tic-Tac-Toe

Player Names

First Player (X)

Second Player (O)





Tic-Tac-Toe is a two-player game played on a 3x3 grid. Players take turns marking a square (either X or O) until one player successfully places three of their marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row. The player who achieves this first wins the game. If all squares are filled without a winner, the game results in a draw.
