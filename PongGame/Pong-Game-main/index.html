Pong Game

Player 1: 0 - Player 2: 0

Player 1: Control with W and S keys

Player 2: Control with up and down arrow keys

Game Rules: Pong is a two-player game where each player controls a paddle. Player 1 uses the W and S keys, while Player 2 uses the arrow keys. The goal is to hit the ball past your opponent's paddle to score points. First player to reach a certain score wins the game.
